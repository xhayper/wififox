WiFiFox

A menubar app for bypassing WiFi login pages.

FEATURES

Connect Without Login

Works in thousands of open networks - the kind at coffee shops, airports, and malls.

Protect Your Privacy

Don't give away your email and personal details just for internet access.

Skip The Wait

Dodge annoying wait times, intrusive ads and long registration/signup forms.

HOW IT WORKS

WiFiFox scans the network to see who is already connected and registered.

It then borrows another "MAC address" -
safely bypassing any login pages.

Curious for details? The code is 100% open-source.
